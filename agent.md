# dotsai.in Agent Context

## Project
- Brand: `dotsai.in`
- Owner positioning: solo entrepreneur AI agency / productized AI studio
- Core offer today: private AI for businesses and solopreneurs
- Target outcome: revamp the current website into a premium single-page landing experience with compartment-based scrolling and scroll-driven storytelling
- Primary growth goal: make `dotsai.in` highly discoverable in India for commercial-intent searches around `AI Agency`, `AI Consulting`, `Private AI`, and adjacent service terms

## User Intent
- Keep the same broad hero direction and overall theme
- Upgrade the visual quality to feel premium, sharp, and high-trust
- Avoid generic "bento box" SaaS layouts
- Use one single landing page with distinct scroll compartments / chapters
- Each compartment should have its own animation logic that progresses with scrolling before handing off to the next section
- Services may later split into dedicated subdomains such as:
  - `geo.dotsai.in`
  - `private.dotsai.in`
  - `platform.dotsai.in`
  - `web.dotsai.in`
- Current stage: ideation and research, not implementation
- SEO priority: strong indexing, crawlability, topical authority, and service architecture that can scale

## Positioning Hypothesis
- dotsai should read less like a generic AI agency and more like a high-conviction solo operator who designs and builds private AI systems, internal AI products, and applied automations for serious businesses
- The website should communicate:
  - founder-led expertise
  - privacy / trust
  - custom systems over commodity prompts
  - fast execution
  - clear business outcomes

## Research Goals
1. Audit the current public website and extract what should stay vs change.
2. Research premium single-page storytelling patterns with compartment scrolling.
3. Research scroll-animation implementation approaches that are performant and maintainable.
4. Research positioning patterns for solo consultant / boutique AI studio websites.
5. Evaluate information architecture options:
   - one page only
   - one page plus subpages
   - service subdomains vs subfolders
6. Research current best practices for Google indexing, technical SEO, internal linking, and India-relevant organic visibility.
7. Research how to run first-party analytics, attribution, consent, and traffic logging on a VPS-hosted PostgreSQL setup.
8. Produce markdown research files that can guide design, copy, IA, SEO, measurement, and later build execution.

## Working Constraints
- Must preserve brand continuity rather than fully rebrand
- Must feel premium without looking like a trendy template
- Must support future expansion into service-specific properties
- Must work for both enterprise buyers and high-value founder / SMB buyers
- Must remain understandable quickly above the fold
- Must not sacrifice indexability or content depth for visual effects
- Must balance a premium single-page experience with the need for search-entry pages and service-level relevance

## Deliverables
- `agent.md` with working context
- research markdown files covering:
  - current site audit
  - positioning
  - landing page structure
  - scroll / animation patterns
  - service architecture recommendations
  - indexing / technical SEO recommendations
  - analytics / attribution / consent recommendations
  - implementation notes
