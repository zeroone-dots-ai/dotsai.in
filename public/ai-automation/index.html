// where automation wins

High-frequency, judgment-light tasks

The strongest candidates are repetitive decisions with known rules, structured escalation, and heavy context lookup. That is where AI agents and workflow layers can save time without creating operational chaos.

Human review where it matters

Not every automation should be fully autonomous. The right model is often AI-prepared output plus clear human approval at the highest-risk points.

AI automation for Indian MSMEs, startups, and growing businesses

Most AI automation tools are designed for US enterprise IT departments with dedicated ops teams and six-figure software budgets. Indian MSMEs and startups face different constraints: tight headcount, mixed-language data, DPDP Act compliance obligations, and workflows built on WhatsApp, email, and custom ERPs rather than Salesforce and Jira.

ZeroOne D.O.T.S AI builds custom AI automation systems that fit this reality. Solutions are scoped to your actual workflow — not a generic template — and deployed on infrastructure you control, so there is no per-seat SaaS bill that grows faster than the value you get from it.
