ANSWERS

The questions founders actually ask.

How is ZeroOne different from ChatGPT, Claude, or a generic AI consultant? +

Think about Day 1 with GPT or Claude versus today. Those are tools everyone else also has. We build business-specific systems fine-tuned on your data — so they keep getting sharper with use. Your model, your weights, your moat.

What does "Private AI" actually mean? +

Your data stays in your environment. Your model is yours. No shared inference endpoints, no training on your data by third parties. We build on isolated infrastructure — VPC, on-prem, or air-gapped — when compliance or sensitivity requires it. Aamdhanee's bank-statement analyser runs this way.

Yes. The industry charges 15–25% of build cost in annual maintenance — every year, for the life of the system. ZeroOne charges that number zero. We ship it, we support it, we retrain it.
