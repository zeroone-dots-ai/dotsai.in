6 currencies.
One fair rate.

All prices in this catalog are listed in INR and USD. Other regional bands apply a currency multiplier to the USD anchor. Contact us to get your region's rate.

INR prices are the base. USD is the international anchor. UAE, UK, Indonesia, and South Africa use regional multipliers applied to USD — these are scoped per engagement. Final numbers in a 45-minute call.
