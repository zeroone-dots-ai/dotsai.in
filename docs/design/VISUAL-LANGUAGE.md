# Visual Language

## Direction: "Paper / Ink / Plum"

Editorial technology atelier. Premium solopreneur aesthetic.
Think: a brilliant craftsperson's studio, not a startup's landing page.

## Color Usage

### 80% — Paper & Ink (light body sections)
- Background: `#F7F3ED` (warm cream — NOT cold white)
- Text: `#171722` (near black ink)
- Muted text: `#6E675F`

### Hero section (dark)
- Background: `#06060a` deep black
- Gradient: `#171722 → #241D33` (plum-dark)

### 15% — Brand Accents
- Deep Plum `#43305F` — primary accent for CTAs, hover states
- Lavender `#D7CFF0` — H1 italic, secondary highlights
- Gold `#B28743` — ONLY in proof/metrics section (premium signal)

### 5% — D.O.T.S Signal Colors (galaxy + cursor ONLY)
- Lavender `#D7CFF0` — Data
- Mint `#C9DED4` — Operations
- Peach `#F1C6AE` — Tech
- Sky `#BFD6EC` — Strategy

## Typography Scale

| Element | Font | Size | Weight |
|---------|------|------|--------|
| H1 hero | Instrument Serif | clamp(64px, 10vw, 120px) | 400 |
| H2 section | Instrument Serif | clamp(36px, 5vw, 64px) | 400 |
| Eyebrow | Space Mono | 11px, 0.22em tracking | 400 |
| Body | DM Sans | clamp(16px, 2vw, 19px) | 300 |
| Nav | Space Mono | 11px | 400 |
| Labels | Space Mono | 10–11px | 400 |

## What NOT To Do
- ❌ Cold white backgrounds (#fff) — use warm cream
- ❌ Glassmorphism / frosted glass
- ❌ Neon colors / gradients
- ❌ Bento grid layouts
- ❌ Card-based services section
- ❌ Cyberpunk / dark tech aesthetic
- ❌ Contact forms (direct contact only)
